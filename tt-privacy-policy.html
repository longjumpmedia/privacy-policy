Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

At Longjump Media, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the TT mobile app ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

TT is designed for users of all ages, and we do not collect any personal information. Users download and access the App through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, which may collect data per their own privacy policies.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Location data
  • Device-specific identifiers tied to personal identity

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to understand general usage trends (e.g., number of users, gameplay frequency).

2. How We Use Information

Any non-personal, anonymous data is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and gameplay experience
  • Understand general user engagement trends
  • Serve advertisements in the freemium version of the app

3. Advertising

The freemium version of TT displays third-party ads through ad networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect non-personal information (like approximate location or device type) to deliver relevant ads. We do not control third-party privacy practices and recommend reviewing their policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

TT is family-friendly and safe for all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, the app is compliant with child privacy standards, including COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data, we still implement industry-standard security measures to protect the app and any anonymized usage data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date and provide in-app or website notification, if necessary.
